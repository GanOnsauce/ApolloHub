ApolloHub

ApolloHub is a simple, fun way to play games in the simplest syntax, HTML. No funny, fancy language. Just in your browser. Thank you to legitfrodo & ApolloSMH For being a test subject for this experiment. We hope you enjoy this small project taking form since April of 2021. (How to Download below /.

How To Download


Head to the top of the page and download the zip file,
Open it in your file (CHROME OS), finder (MAC OS) or file explorer (WINDOWS) app.
Find the file whitch should be named ApolloHub-main.zip
Open the file named ApolloHub_Client.html Or ApolloHubClient.js (for the dedicated) , the instructions are also there too before you launch your game
